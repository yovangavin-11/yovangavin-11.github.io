About Chickens

Chickens are basically tiny dinosaurs with better fashion sense. They strut around like they own the place (because they do), wake everyone up at unreasonable hours, and somehow still expect snacks.

Despite their reputation for crossing roads without explanation, chickens are actually quite clever. Some can even recognize faces — so be nice… they remember EVERYTHING.

Chicken Joke: Why did the chicken join a band? Because it had the drumsticks! 🥁

Fun Chicken Facts

  • Chickens can run up to 9 miles per hour… which is faster than you chasing one.
  • They dream when they sleep. Probably about corn. Definitely about corn.
  • A group of chickens is called a "flock"… or "organized chaos."
  • Some chickens can remember over 100 different faces. Yes, they know what you did.
  • There are more chickens on Earth than humans. They’re just letting us think we’re in charge.
